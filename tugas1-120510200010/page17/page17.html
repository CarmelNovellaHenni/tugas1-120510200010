How to measure muscular endurance

There are some way to measure muscular endurance.


Muscular endurance tests measure how many repetitions of a movement people can do before the muscles reach a state of fatigue and cannot continue the exercise. Many tests focus on measuring upper and lower body muscle endurance by measuring how many pushups, squats, or situps people can achieve. A person can work with fitness instructors to measure muscular endurance or record how many repetitions of a particular exercise they can perform before reaching the fatigue state.
